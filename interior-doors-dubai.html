1. Types of Interior Doors in Dubai

There are several types of interior doors available in Dubai, including:

Hinged Doors
Hinged doors are the most common type of interior door in Dubai. They come in a variety of designs, sizes, and materials, and they swing open and close on hinges attached to the door frame.

Sliding Doors
Sliding doors are an excellent option for small spaces as they don't require the same amount of space to open as hinged doors. They come in various designs and materials, including glass and wood.

Pocket Doors
Pocket doors are a type of sliding door that slide into a pocket or cavity in the wall when opened. They are an excellent option for spaces where you don't want a visible door, such as closets.

Materials Used in Interior Doors in Dubai

Interior doors in Dubai come in several different materials, including:

Wood
Wood is the most popular material used in interior doors in Dubai. It's versatile, durable, and comes in a range of designs and finishes to suit any style.

Glass
Glass doors are an excellent option for homeowners who want to let more light into their home. They come in a range of styles, including frosted, textured, and stained glass.

Aluminum
Aluminum doors are a modern option that is lightweight, durable, and corrosion-resistant. They come in a range of designs and finishes to suit any style.

How to Choose the Right Interior Door for Your Home

When choosing an interior door for your home, consider the following factors:

Style
Choose a door that matches the style of your home. For example, if you have a modern home, choose a door with a sleek and simple design.

Material
Choose a material that suits your needs. For example, if you want a durable and long-lasting door, choose one made of wood or aluminum.

Size
Ensure that the door you choose fits the space you have available.

Maintaining Interior Doors in Dubai

To ensure that your interior doors last for many years, you need to maintain them correctly. Here are some tips:

Regular Cleaning
Regularly clean your doors with a damp cloth to remove any dirt or dust.

Lubrication
Lubricate the hinges and locks of your doors regularly to ensure they operate smoothly.

Avoid Slamming
Avoid slamming your doors, as this can damage the hinges and frame.
