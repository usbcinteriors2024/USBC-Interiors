AC Maintenance Dubai: Common Issues


Regular AC maintenance can prevent several common issues that occur in AC units.

Here are a few examples below

Low refrigerant levels
Low refrigerant levels can cause the AC unit to blow warm air instead of cold air. This problem is typically caused by leaks, which can be challenging to detect without professional help.
Dirty air filters
Dirty air filters can reduce the airflow in the AC unit, which can lead to poor indoor air quality and reduced cooling efficiency.
Faulty thermostat
A faulty thermostat can cause the AC unit to malfunction, leading to temperature inconsistencies and poor cooling performance.

AC Maintenance Dubai: Best Practices

To keep your AC unit running smoothly,

follow these best practices for AC maintenance in Dubai:

Schedule Regular Maintenance Regular AC maintenance should be scheduled at least once a year. It's best to have your unit serviced before the summer season to ensure optimal performance. Clean or Replace Air Filters Air filters should be cleaned or replaced every three months to prevent dust and dirt buildup. Check Refrigerant Levels Check refrigerant levels regularly to ensure they are at the correct level. Low refrigerant levels can lead to poor cooling performance. Clean Condenser Coils Condenser coils should be cleaned at least once a year to prevent dirt and debris buildup, which can reduce the unit's efficiency. Check Electrical Connections Loose or damaged electrical connections can cause the AC unit to malfunction. Check electrical connections regularly and tighten any loose connections. Keep the Outdoor Unit Clean The outdoor unit should be kept clean and free from debris, such as leaves and dirt. Debris can block the airflow and reduce the unit's efficiency.
