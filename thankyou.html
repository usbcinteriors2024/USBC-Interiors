AT USBC INTERIORS

The leadership team at USBC interiors (Urban Science Building Contracting) have been involved in several 'Creative Interior design' turn key fit-out projects in Dubai, which includes design and execution of the entire space. Through the involvement of creative architects, experienced joinery team, state of art carpentry facility in Dubai and in-house MEP engineers, USBC is well placed to become one of the leading interior fit-out companies.

USBC interiors came into conception to address the need of the clients for those quality and finish is extremely important with time as a vital parameter. To address those requirements we decided to establish joinery workshops with latest technologies along with a team of engineers with vast experience in building services such as air conditioning, electrical, plumbing and IT solutions leaving no loose ends before being delivered to our prestigious clients.

For USBC Interiors, there is no job 'small or big', its all unique in nature and therefore we undertake projects of any type such as Exhibitions, Carpentry & Joinery, renovation of hotels, villas, offices and restaurants. Our mission is to provide a comprehensive range of luxury fit-out for Living rooms, pools/landscape and vibrant designs of kitchen refurbishments.

USBC Interiors is specialized in turnkey Interior Fit-outs for residential & commercial spaces in Dubai
