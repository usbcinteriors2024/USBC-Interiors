How much does it cost for interior design in Dubai? +

In general, you can expect to pay between AED 70 and AED 300 per square foot for interior design services in Dubai. This price range includes the cost of the designer's fees as well as the cost of materials and finishes.

How much do interior designers get paid in Dubai? +

The average salary for an interior designer in Dubai is around 18,000 to 20,000 AED per month. This can range from 9,000 to 30,000 AED, depending on your experience and qualifications
