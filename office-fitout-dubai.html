Our Fit-Out Process


PREPARATION & BRIEFING
We find out all of your project's needs and goals over a cup of coffee in our material library and think tank. In order to ensure a smooth project launch, our team will also compile the relevant site information and documentation.
CONCEPT DESIGN
Using the data acquired during the planning and briefing phase, our team will develop a concept during this phase. We work together on a regular basis to develop a concept that is genuinely tailored to your needs in this flexible process. By the time this phase is over, you will have a complete visual representation of your design in the form of several 3D renders, furniture arrangements, preliminary material specifications, and a fit-out work budget.
SPECIAL COORDINATION
In this phase, we carry out the agreed-upon design in every part of the room, choose real finishes from real samples, and present an updated and more thorough cost plan for the fit-out tasks.
TECHNICAL DESIGN
Our technical design team will create the Issue For Construction pack (IFC) at this stage, which will include drawings, FFE specifications, and detailed layouts (or BIM model, if needed) that will enable the construction team to carry out the fit-out precisely as planned. In addition, we will complete the fit-out work cost plan and get all required paperwork ready for submission in order to obtain building permits and mandatory approvals.

How long an office fitout work takes for completion?+

The size of the space, the complexity of the design, and the amount of work involved are some of the variables that affect how long an office fit-out project takes. A small- to medium-sized office fit-out often takes several weeks to many months. A comprehensive schedule will be furnished during the preliminary project planning phases.

What distinguishes office interior design from office interior fit out?+
Office Interior Design vs. Office Interior Fit Out While architects and fit out teams are responsible for making sure they have all the building permits and approvals needed to complete the work, interior designers consider the arrangement and structure of the space. The goal of interior design is to create a functioning place.
