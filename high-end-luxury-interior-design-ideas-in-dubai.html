FAQ

1. What is the average cost of luxury interior design in Dubai?

The cost of luxury interior design in Dubai can vary greatly depending on the size of the space, the materials used, and the complexity of the design. However, on average, it can range from AED 500 to AED 2,500 per square meter.

2. How long does it take to complete a luxury interior design project in Dubai?

The duration of a luxury interior design project in Dubai can also vary depending on the size and complexity of the project. However, on average, it can take anywhere from three to twelve months to complete a project.

3. How can I choose the right luxury interior design firm in Dubai?

When choosing a luxury interior design firm in Dubai, it's essential to do your research and look for a company that has experience designing similar spaces to yours. It's also important to check their portfolio, read client reviews, and make sure they are licensed and insured.

4. What are some popular luxury interior design styles in Dubai?

Some popular luxury interior design styles in Dubai include classic, contemporary, modern, and Arabic. Each style has its unique characteristics, and it's important to work with an interior designer who can help you choose the right style for your space.

5. How can I incorporate sustainable design into my luxury interior design project in Dubai?

Incorporating sustainable design elements into a luxury interior design project in Dubai can help reduce environmental impact while also creating a beautiful and functional living space. Some ways to incorporate sustainable design include using eco-friendly materials, choosing energy-efficient appliances and lighting, and incorporating greenery and natural light into the design.
